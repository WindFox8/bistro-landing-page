About us

Located in the heart of the city, Bistrô Lumiere was born out of a passion for sharing special moments around good food. Founded in 1997, the bistro is the result of hard work and dedication from our talented team, committed to creating a unique atmosphere for gastronomy enthusiasts. At Bistrô Lumiere, we believe that every meal is a celebration, and we look forward to welcoming you to our charming space. Whether it's for a special occasion, a gathering of friends, or a romantic evening, we are here to make each moment unforgettable.
